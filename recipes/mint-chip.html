Mint Chip

Description:

Mint is just one of those classic recipes you see in ALL the ice cream parlors you visited as a kid.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cup whole milk
  • 1 cup of white sugar
  • Pinch of salt
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 2 teaspoon of mint extract
  • 4 drops of green food coloring
  • 6 ounces of semisweet chocolate

Steps to make:

  1. In a medium bowl, whisk to combine the milk, sugar, and salt until the sugar is dissolved. Stir in the heavy cream and mint extract. Lastly for coloring for desired color. Cover and refrigerate 4 hours.
  2. Turn on the ICE-100 maker; pour the mixture into the bowl and let mix until thickened, about 15 to 20 minutes. When finished add the chocolate to mix it into the ice cream. The ice cream will have a soft creamy texture, almost like a frosty
  3. If a firmer consistency is desired, transfer the ice cream to an airtight container and place in freezer overnight.
  4. Remove from freezer about 15 minutes before serving!s
