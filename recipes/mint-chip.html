Vanilla Ice Cream

Description:

This ice cream can easily be dressed up by adding your favorite candies or sprinkles!

The recipe is a classic for all age groups using organic ingredients and also a classic way of making ice cream

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Organic whole milk
  • 3/4 cup of white sugar
  • Pinch of salt
  • 2 cups Organic heavy cream
  • 1 tablespoon of vanilla beans

Steps to make:

  1. In a medium bowl, whisk to combine the milk, sugar, and salt until the sugar is dissolved. Stir in the heavy cream and vanilla. Cover and refrigerate 4 hours.
  2. Turn on the ICE-100 maker; pour the mixture into the bowl and let mix until thickened, about 15 to 20 minutes. The ice cream will have a soft creamy texture, almost like a frosty
  3. If a firmer consistency is desired, transfer the ice cream to an airtight container and place in freezer overnight.
  4. Remove from freezer about 15 minutes before serving!s
