Creamy Chocolate Gelato

Description:

This gelato is an ancient Italian recipe passed down from my great great grandmother!

It will knock your socks off with how delicious this gelato is!

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of whole milk
  • 3/4 cup of white sugar
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1/3 cups of heavy cream
  • 1/3 cup unsweetened cocoa powder

Steps to make:

  1. In a medium bowl, whisk to combine the milk, sugar, and salt until the sugar is dissolved. Stir in the heavy cream and cocoa powder. Cover and refrigerate 4 hours.
  2. Turn on the ICE-100 maker; pour the mixture into the bowl and let mix until thickened, about 15 to 20 minutes. The ice cream will have a soft creamy texture, almost like a frosty
  3. If a firmer consistency is desired, transfer the ice cream to an airtight container and place in freezer overnight.
  4. Remove from freezer about 15 minutes before serving!
